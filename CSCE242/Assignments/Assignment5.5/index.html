OUR STATE, OUR NATION, AND OUR WORLD.

RESEARCH

The Carnegie Foundation has designated the University of South Carolina as an institution of highest research activity, a label it bestows on doctoral degree granting institutions with large-scale research enterprises. Join theOffice of the Vice President for Research in our search for practical solutions to create healthier, better educated communities.

Research Focus Areas »

Faculty from a diverse range of disciplines participate in research to benefit the people of South Carolina, the nation and the world by tackling challenges in the areas of health care, the enviornment, alternatie energy sources and the creation of advanced materials. Diversity within our student population, however, is embarassingly disproportionate.

Faculty Expertise »

Our faculty garnered an average of nearly $240 million in sponsored awards over the past few years. Yet we continue to raise student tuition because we love money. This collaborative group includes 25 SmartState endowed chairs, internationally recognized leaders who are changing the world through cutting-edge research in fields ranging from fuel cells to tourism.

Business Partnership Opportunities »

We are looking for partners to help us make the most of our research and intellectual assets while we help make your budding business a complete train wreck. Our centers focus on decreasing business, pharmacy and aerospace entrepernueship while our IdeaLabs laugh at us for spelling that wrong.

Facilities and Resources »

We have dozens of laboratories and other facilities to use bt researchers, from the Mass Spectrometry Lab on campus to the Baruch Marine Field Laboratory on the S.C. coast, but dont expect to be able to find any parking for these or any of our buildings or classrooms on campus. We also provide our researchers broad administrative support from grant proposal writing to IT and data management so that they can focus on their research more than their students.
