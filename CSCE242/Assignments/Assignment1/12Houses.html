The 12 Houses

Astrologie's Zodiac is divided into 12 segments called Houses. Each House is rued by a different sign. Each House represents a different set of traits. The zodiac begins at the 1st house and moves counterclockwise to the 12th.

The signs that rule the 12 houses are:

  • Aries
  • Taurus
  • Gemini
  • Cancer
  • Leo
  • Virgo
  • Libra
  • Scorpio
  • Sagittarius
  • Capricorn
  • Aquarius
  • Pisces
  1. 1st House

    2. Covers all 'firsts'. i.e. impressions, beginnings, leadership, initiatives, etc. The sign on the cusp of this house is referred to as your rising sign. This house is ruled by Aries

  2. 2nd House

    3. Concerns matters relating to your immediate physical enviornment and the 5 senses. It rules self-esteem, income, and money. The 2nd House is ruled by Taurus.

  3. 3rd House

    4. Ruled by Gemini, this House relates to all forms of communication such as talking, thinking, virtual, etc. as well as neighborhoods, libraries, schools, local travel, teachers, sibings, and community affairs.

  4. 4th House

    5. This house sits at the bottom of the wheel and represents the foundation of all things including your home, security, privacy, and parents. It is ruled by Cancer

  5. 5th House

    6. Ruled by Leo, this house governs drama, romance, creativity, attention, fun, and color.

  6. 6th House

    7. Concerns all to do with health and service, including schedules, diet and excersize, routines, natural and healty living, helpfulness, etc. It is ruled by Virgo

  7. 7th House

    8. This house, ruled by Libra, relates to relationships, partnerships, contracts, marriages, and other business deals.

  8. 8th House

    9. This mysterious house ruled by Scorpio concerns birth, death, transformation, sex, ysteries, bonding, and merged energies, as well as other people's money and property.

  9. 9th House

    10. This house covers expansion, the higher mind, higher education, foreign languages, inspiration, optimism, broadcasting, gambling, adventurs, philosophy, morals, ethics religion, long distance traveling, luck, and risk. It is ruled by Sagittarius

  10. 10th House

    11. At the top and most public part of the wheel, this house ruled by Capricorn governs structures, tradition, corporations, public image, honor, fame, authority, discipline, fatherhood, awards, boundaries, etc.

  11. 11th House

    12. This house covers all to do with friendships, society, technology, media, rebellion, networking, social justice, originality, eccentricity, suprises, invention, and all things futuristic. It is ruled by Aquarius

  12. 12th House

    13. The final House, ruled by Pisces, governs endings, tying up loose ends, hidden agendas, hospitals, jails, and secret enemies.
